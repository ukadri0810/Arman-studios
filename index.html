The photographer

More observer than director.

I photograph people as they are—elegant, expressive and fully present in the life unfolding around them.

My approach balances fashion-informed portraiture with documentary storytelling. I guide when needed, then step back to let the real rhythm of the day take over.

Based in Mumbai and available worldwide, I work with couples, creatives and brands who value imagery that feels timeless rather than trend-led.
